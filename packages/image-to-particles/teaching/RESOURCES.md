# p5.js Resources

## Knowledge

- [p5.js Reference](https://p5js.org/reference/)
  The official API docs. Bookmark this — you'll use it daily.
- [The Coding Train / Daniel Shiffman — p5.js videos](https://thecodingtrain.com/)
  The best video introduction to p5.js and generative algorithms. Shiffman's _Nature of Code_ playlist is directly relevant to particle systems.
- [Book: _The Nature of Code_ — Daniel Shiffman](https://natureofcode.com/)
  Free online. Teaches simulation — vectors, forces, particles, steering, physics. The foundation for your image-to-particles vision.
- [p5.js Examples](https://p5js.org/examples/)
  Official examples: hello-p5.js, load-display-image, pointillism-effect.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/generative](https://reddit.com/r/generative)
  Active community posting algorithmic art. Good for inspiration and "how would I approach X" questions.
- [OpenProcessing](https://openprocessing.org/)
  Sketches you can remix. Search "particle" or "flow field" for thousands of examples.
- [The Nature of Code Discord](https://discord.gg/natureofcode)
  Community around Shiffman's book. Friendly to beginners.
