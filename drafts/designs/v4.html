Accordion

            

              Native details/summary. Zero JS. @starting-style animates content.
            

            

              

                
                  what CSS features power this library?
                
                

                  :has(), :focus-within, details/summary, dialog, popover, color-mix(),
                  @starting-style, CSS nesting, @layer, accent-color, field-sizing, scroll-snap,
                  text-wrap: balance/pretty
                

              

              

                
                  why oklch for colors?
                
                

                  perceptually uniform. equal lightness changes look equal to humans. makes
                  generating palettes and color-mix() results predictable.
                

              

            

            

              

                components/Accordion.tsx
